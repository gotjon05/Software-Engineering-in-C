++wordlen[nchar]; vs. ++nchar;

Difference bettwen incrementing the index and incrementing variable of array(confused by incrementing array variable ex: ++wordlen[nchar];)
	https://stackoverflow.com/questions/7595247/array-increment-types-in-c-arrayi-vs-arrayi

	++wordlen[nchar]	 
		increments the value at nchar index 

	wordlen[++nchar] or in this program, nchar++
		increments the index
